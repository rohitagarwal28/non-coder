TASK-when we start a linux system the screen will show the files/directories in download folder.
SOLUTION- 
1) Open home and press ctrl+H to show the hidden files.
2) Now open the .config folder
3) Then open user-dirs.dirs file.
4) Now you will get this statement "XDG_DESKTOP_DIR="$HOME/Desktop"".in this statement replace 'desktop'  with 'downloads'
5) Then at last reboot your system.
